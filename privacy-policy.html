Privacy Policy

At Hamiko, accessible from https://hamiko.com, one of our main priorities is protecting the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what types of information we collect and record and how we use it. If you have any questions or require further information, feel free to contact us at privacy@hamiko.com. This Privacy Policy applies only to our online activities and is valid for visitors to our website regarding the information they share or that we collect through the site. It does not apply to offline data collection or channels outside this website.

Consent

By using our website, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Information We Collect

The information you provide and why it is needed will be made clear when we ask for it. This may include:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and company name.
  • Messages: Content of your message, attachments, or other details you share when contacting us.
  • Account Registration: Information like name, address, email address, and phone number if you register for an account.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Operate and maintain our website.
  • Personalize and improve your experience.
  • Understand how visitors use our website.
  • Develop new services and features.
  • Communicate with you for customer support, updates, marketing, and promotions.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security.

Log Files

Hamiko follows standard practices for using log files. These include data such as:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser type and version.
  • Internet Service Provider (ISP).
  • Date and time of visits.
  • Referring/exit pages.
  • Number of clicks.

This information is used for analytics, trend analysis, and website optimization, and it is not linked to personally identifiable information.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Store visitor preferences.
  • Track website usage.
  • Customize content based on your browser type or other data.

For general information about cookies, refer to What Are Cookies.

Advertising Partners Privacy Policies

Third-party ad servers or networks may use cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons on our website. These are used to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns or to personalize ads you see. Note that Hamiko does not control these cookies used by third-party advertisers.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

Hamiko's Privacy Policy does not cover other websites or advertisers. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more details, including how to manage cookies or opt-out.

CCPA Privacy Rights (Do Not Sell My Personal Information)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Request the categories and specific personal data we collect.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-out of the sale of your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@hamiko.com

GDPR Data Protection Rights

We ensure you are fully aware of your data protection rights:

  • Right to Access: Request copies of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data under certain conditions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limited use of your data under certain conditions.
  • Right to Object to Processing: Object to our processing of your data under certain conditions.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another organization or directly to you.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@hamiko.com

Children’s Information

Protecting children online is a priority for us. We encourage parents and guardians to monitor and guide their children’s internet use. Hamiko does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided such information, contact us immediately, and we will promptly remove it.

For more information, feel free to reach out to us at privacy@hamiko.com
